ADIDAS SHOES

Also known as AG or Adidas Group in the sportswear industry, Adidas is a German-owned global company best known for its athletic footwear, sportswear, and other sporting merchandise. The company began in 1924 when it was founded by Adolf Dassler and his brother Rudolf under the name Dassler Brothers Shoe Factory.
